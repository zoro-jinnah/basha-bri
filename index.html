HYDERABADI CHICKEN BRIYANI

Hyderabadi Biryani originating from Golconda Hyderabad, India made with Kuchchi basmati rice and Chicken Originated in the kitchens of the Qutub Shahi Kingdom, it combines elements of Qutub Shahi and later on Asif Jahi cuisines. Hyderabad biryani is a key dish flourished during the period of the Nizam of Hyderabad and it is so famous that the dish is considered synonymous with the city of Hyderabad.

AMBUR BRIYANI

Biryani has been an integral part of Ambur Culture and tradition for hundreds of years. It is believed to have been travelled directly from Persia. Ambur Biryani, the key dish of the South indian kitchens reached Ambor after the Mughal Emperor appointed Subedar Nawab Saadatullah Khan Bahaduras the new ruler of Arcot Province. Now it has become very popular Banquet Dish all over the region.
  Made with Superior Dehradan Basmati Rice along with Spices, Onion, Ginger, Garlic Chill, Mint Coriander and Lemon, Dircetly Cooked in Copper Deghchi heated above Tamarind Firewood along with Proper Dumn Desa Process Blend the exact flavour Taste Buds Craves
